technoeconomic costs tool

  • status: ongoing
  • description: a tool for projecting investment costs and fixed operational & maintenance costs of technologies. currently being used for MESSAGEix development.
  • more info: open-source and housed under the message-ix-models package

CHILLED

  • status: ongoing
  • description: a model that projects the impacts of climate change on residential energy demand. currently being applied within projects at IIASA at the global level.
  • more info: open-source and housed under the message-ix-buildings package

weather and electricity prices

  • status: completed
  • description: analysis of impacts of weather variables (such as temperature) on grid prices

india electricity model

  • status: ongoing
  • description: india's electricity grid futures under different economic and policy scenarios.
  • more info: uses an open-source GIS-based multi-criteria siting tool for renewable energy called MapRE and an open-source electricity modeling tool called GridPath

equitably reaching net-zero within california's transportation fuel supply sector

  • status: ongoing
  • description: net-zero pathways and the impacts on health and labor for the transportation fossil fuel supply sector in california
  • more info: nature energy paper published for the extraction side (along with code base), as well as a report for both extraction and refining

water for coal

  • status: completed
  • description: spatially-resolved life cycle water usage for coal-fired electricity production
  • more info: paper published in energy technology

temperature and power plants
